Why Study in Latvia?

Here are some reasons why you should consider studying in Latvia:

  • Students can work part-time for up to 20 hours per week.
  • All programs are taught in English.
  • All universities are government or government-approved.
  • 3-year bachelor's students can pursue a master's degree if they score above the 2nd division in their bachelor's.
  • IELTS is not required if the student can demonstrate proficiency in English.
  • Conditional offer letters take approximately 4-5 working days, while non-conditional offer letters take around 20 days.
  • General BA/B.Sc degrees and correspondence degrees are not acceptable for master's degree applications in Latvia, except for MBA.
About Turiba University

Turiba University is one of the leading higher educational institutions in Latvia with a stable tradition and a clear vision of a common European educational space. Founded in 1993, Turiba is proud of its more than 5800 students and 11,432 alumni. The study program Management of Tourism and Hospitality Companies has received accreditation from the UN/WTO TedQual certificate. Turiba is the first higher education institution in the Baltic region and Scandinavian countries to receive accreditation from the World Tourism Organization and high evaluations from internationally recognized experts.
English Level

Admission Requirements

Here are the admission requirements for Turiba University:

  • Completed application form.
  • Copy of the passport (personal details page).
  • Copy of educational certificates and transcripts.
  • Curriculum vitae (CV).
  • Letter of motivation.
  • English language proficiency certificate (if available).
  • Bank statement showing sufficient funds to cover tuition and living expenses.
  • Application fee payment confirmation.

For SPOT Assessment: Kindly bring all your original documents and a photocopy set - 10th & 12th Mark sheets, Bachelor Degree & Mark sheets Curriculum Vitae, Work Experience Certificates (if applicable), TOEFL/IELTS/PTE (if taken), Passport, Photograph.
Scholarships

Turiba University offers scholarships for outstanding international students. The scholarships cover up to 50% of the tuition fee. To be eligible for a scholarship, students must meet certain academic criteria and submit a scholarship application along with their admission application.
