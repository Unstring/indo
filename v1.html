Study in Latvia

Turiba University

Turiba University is one of the leading higher educational institutions in Latvia with a stable tradition and a clear vision of the common European educational space. Founded in 1993, Turiba is proud of its more than 5800 students and 11,432 alumni.

Why study in Latvia?

  • Student can work part-time for 20 hours a week.
  • All programs are taught in English.
  • All universities are government or government-approved.
  • 3-year bachelor's students can pursue a master's degree if they scored more than 2nd division in their bachelor's.
  • IELTS is not required if students can speak English.
  • Conditional offer letters take approximately 4-5 working days.
  • Non-conditional offer letters take approximately 20 days.
  • General BA/B.Sc degrees and correspondence degrees are not acceptable for master's degree in Latvia, except for MBA.
